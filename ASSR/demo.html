Robotic Society of RUET

Robotic Society of RUET (RSR) is a place for robotics and automation students, enthusiasts and hobbyists from RUET. It facilitates collaboration, skill sharing and learning among the members focusing both professional skill development and competitive programs organized around the country and abroad.
